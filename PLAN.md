# SZLL Team Page Plan

## Visual thesis

A bright organizer-facing team dossier: handmade event-paper texture, strong borders, clear member responsibility, and enough Busan/DIVE character to feel local without hiding the team behind decoration.

## Audience

Program coordinators and organizers reviewing whether the team is complete, understandable, manageable, and suitable for a two-day hackathon format.

## Content plan

1. Hero: make `SZLL` the only team name and summarize why the team is ready for DIVE 2026.
2. Organizer summary: show event fit, format, team mix, and member geography at a glance.
3. Members: present each person with role, affiliation, background, strengths, and expected hackathon contribution.
4. Team building: explain how the team will operate, communicate, and divide work.
5. Readiness: group technical and creative capabilities into organizer-friendly categories.
6. Contact: give one clear primary contact for review and coordination.

## Interaction thesis

1. Subtle entrance animation on member records to make the roster feel active.
2. Tactile hover movement on member blocks, like physical dossiers on a table.
3. A compact member index in the hero helps coordinators understand the team immediately.

## Notes

- Phone numbers from `team-info.md` are intentionally not displayed on the public page.
- The team name is only `SZLL`.
